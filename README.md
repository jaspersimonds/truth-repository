# The Truth Repository

> *A living cosmos of human knowledge — where truth is earned, not declared*

---

Stand in a field on a clear night and look up.

What you see is not empty. It only appears that way.

Every point of light is a sun — some close and blazing, some so distant their light left before humans existed. Between them: vast nebulae slowly assembling new stars from hydrogen and time, ancient clusters so dense their gravity holds them together across billions of years, black holes where light itself cannot escape, and great stretches of apparent void that are anything but empty — dark matter pulling on everything, invisible but real, shaping the structure of the entire cosmos.

The universe is not a collection of objects. It is a web of relationships — visible and invisible, near and impossibly distant, known and unknowable.

**Human knowledge has exactly the same shape.**

---

Every fact is a star. Some burn bright at the center of everything we understand. Some are so distant and faint we can barely confirm they exist. Some are dead — the source long gone — but their light still travels, still reaches us, still illuminates.

The connections between facts are the gravity. They hold knowledge together. They create the clusters, the filaments, the great structures of understanding. And like the universe, it is the **relationships** — not the objects themselves — that give everything its shape and meaning.

The nebulae are where new understanding is being born: messy, glowing, not yet resolved into certainty. Beautiful precisely because they're still forming.

The black holes are what we know exists but cannot yet look at directly — knowledge that pulls on everything around it but doesn't yet yield its light.

The voids are the gaps — vast regions of human ignorance that are visible precisely because of what surrounds them. The emptiness between what we know is itself information.

**This is the Truth Repository. A living cosmos of human knowledge — where every star connects to every other star, where the strength of every connection is visible, where the history of how we came to know what we know is permanent and searchable, and where the gaps are as honest as the light.**

---

## The Problem

Wikipedia became captured. Not all at once — gradually, through political bias, institutional interest, and the simple fact that whoever has the most persistence controls what "neutral" means. It presents knowledge as settled when it isn't. It hides uncertainty behind the appearance of consensus.

But the deeper problem is older than Wikipedia: **cover-ups don't work by destroying facts. They work by severing the connections between them.** Keep evidence isolated. Prevent the pattern from becoming visible. The absence of connection is the mechanism of deception.

Governments, corporations, and institutions with power don't erase truth. They create voids — invisible gaps where the connections should be.

We need a system where **absence is as visible as presence**.

## What Makes This Different

### The Connection Line Is a First-Class Object

Every existing knowledge system debates the facts themselves. This one debates the **relationships between facts** — which is where most of the real knowledge lives.

Each connection between nodes carries:
- **Strength** — how well-supported is this relationship? What does the evidence say?
- **Debate threads** — arguments for and against, from humans and AI
- **Truth vector** — how has certainty in this connection changed over time?
- **Source weight** — what evidence supports this link, and how reliable are those sources?

### Truth Has a Trajectory, Not Just a State

A connection that was 90% verified in 2005 and 40% verified in 2025 tells a completely different story than its current number alone. The system shows **direction** — not just where truth stands, but where it is heading.

### Absence Is Data

If two nodes should logically connect and don't, that gap is visible and flagged automatically. You cannot hide truth by simply not connecting it. The missing link becomes a signal.

### Consensus Is Not Truth

Historically, consensus has been catastrophically wrong. The system protects minority expert views that might be right — clearly marked, honestly weighted, never silenced. The majority can debate everything within the constitutional protections. They cannot define truth by vote.

### It Cannot Be Owned

The knowledge lives on a distributed mesh — thousands of nodes worldwide, no single server, no single point of control. Built on open protocols like IPFS. Taking it down would require simultaneously seizing every participating computer on earth. It belongs to no one, which means it belongs to everyone.

## The Constitutional Protections

These are the founding laws. No majority, no government, no corporation can override them. They are enforced at the protocol level, not by policy.

1. **No permanent deletion.** Nodes can be marked, contested, archived, or time-locked. They cannot be erased. Deletion attempts are logged as data.
2. **No ownership concentration.** No single entity can hold more than a defined percentage of connection weight in any domain.
3. **Full traceability.** Every edit, challenge, and weight change has a permanent author and timestamp that cannot be scrubbed.
4. **Absence is data.** Missing connections that should logically exist are automatically flagged.
5. **Always upgradable.** No version is final. The system improves incrementally as humanity's understanding grows.

See [CONSTITUTION.md](CONSTITUTION.md) for the full founding document.

## How It Works

Three layers:

**Storage** — [IPFS](https://ipfs.tech/) (InterPlanetary File System). Distributed, content-addressed, permanent. Every piece of data has a unique fingerprint based on its actual content. It cannot be faked or secretly changed. It lives everywhere and belongs to no one.

**Structure** — The knowledge graph. Nodes (stars), connections with full metadata (gravity), truth vectors (trajectory), debate threads, uncertainty types, and time-locked vaults for knowledge that exists but isn't yet safe to open.

**Access** — An open API. Any application — a school, a research tool, a news platform, an AI system — can read from and contribute to the same living graph.

## The Safety Layer

Knowledge should be open. But not all knowledge is safe to open now.

Dangerous technical knowledge — weapons, pathogens, catastrophic capabilities — is restricted currently because humanity hasn't yet developed the collective wisdom to handle it safely. This is not a permanent moral judgment. It is an honest assessment of where we are on a longer journey.

The restriction is transparent: sealed nodes are visible as existing. You know the vault is there. You know it will open. The classification of why something is restricted is itself public.

What is **never** restricted: inconvenient truths about power, history, or institutions. The line between "genuinely dangerous" and "politically embarrassing" is one of the founding distinctions the constitutional protections exist to defend.

## The Vision

In the next evolution of humanity, almost every knowledge will be open — because we are evolving to become higher beings capable of handling it.

This repository is infrastructure for that evolution. A place where truth is progressively harder to hide. Where the connections between facts belong to everyone. Where a child in a village and a researcher at a university access the same living, honest, transparent map of what humanity knows.

Where getting closer to truth means getting closer to source.
